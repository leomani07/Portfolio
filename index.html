// About Me

I am an AWS Cloud and DevOps Engineer with experience in designing, deploying, and managing scalable applications on cloud platforms. My expertise includes AWS services such as EC2, VPC, RDS, S3, and IAM, and I specialize in building secure and reliable 3-tier architectures for business applications. On the DevOps side, I work with Terraform for infrastructure as code, Jenkins for CI/CD pipelines, and Docker for containerized applications. I also implement monitoring and observability solutions using Prometheus and Grafana to maintain system health and performance. My focus is on automation, cost optimization, and delivering cloud solutions that are efficient, resilient, and aligned with business goals.What drives me is the challenge of making systems more efficient and resilient. I like solving problems where cloud, automation, and scalability meet. I believe in continuous learning and regularly experiment with new tools, whether it’s improving observability, optimizing costs, or trying out modern DevOps practices. Outside of work, I spend time exploring side projects, contributing to my knowledge of cloud-native technologies, and sharpening my problem-solving skills. My long-term goal is to master advanced DevOps practices and contribute to building highly available, production-grade systems.
